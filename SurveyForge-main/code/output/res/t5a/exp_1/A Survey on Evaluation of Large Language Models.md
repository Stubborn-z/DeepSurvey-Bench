Emerging research highlights the importance of multi-agent and iterative evaluation frameworks. The ChatEval approach, which leverages multi-agent debate mechanisms, represents an innovative strategy for detecting and mitigating hallucinations through collaborative assessment [53]. Semantic compression techniques offer another promising avenue for hallucination mitigation. The evaluation of hallucinations extends beyond traditional metrics, requiring nuanced, context-aware assessment frameworks.
